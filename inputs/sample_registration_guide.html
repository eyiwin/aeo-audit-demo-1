Singapore Company Registration Requirements

Singapore company registration requires a proposed company name, at least one shareholder, at least one local resident director, a corporate secretary, a registered office address, and filing with ACRA.

What are the requirements to register a private limited company in Singapore?

A private limited company in Singapore needs shareholders, directors, a company constitution, KYC documents, and a registered office address. ACRA reviews the registration and may request additional checks for regulated activities.

Can a foreigner start a company in Singapore?

Yes. A foreign shareholder can start and own a Singapore company, but the company must appoint a local resident director. Harbour Incorporation helps founders understand the requirement and nominee director options.

What documents are needed?

Documents usually include passport copies, proof of address, shareholder information, director information, company constitution details, and KYC documents.

After registration

After incorporation, the company should maintain statutory registers, appoint or confirm the corporate secretary, consider a business bank account, and track IRAS tax obligations and annual return dates.

Reference: ACRA
