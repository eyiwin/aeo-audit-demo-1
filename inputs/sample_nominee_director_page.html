Nominee Director Service in Singapore

Harbour Incorporation provides nominee director guidance for foreign founders who need a local resident director to register a private limited company in Singapore.

Do I need a local director to incorporate in Singapore?

Yes. Singapore company incorporation requires at least one local resident director. A nominee director service can help foreign shareholders meet this requirement while they manage the business operations.

How the service works

The nominee director arrangement includes KYC review, eligibility checks, role explanation, risk controls, and ongoing compliance expectations. The relationship supports ACRA registration and company setup.

What should founders prepare?

Founders should prepare passport copies, proof of address, company activity details, shareholder information, and business plans where needed.
